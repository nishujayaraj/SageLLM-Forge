Write a structured evaluation memo with exactly these six sections.

---

SECTION 1 — EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
One paragraph. State clearly whether you recommend PROMOTE, HOLD, or REJECT.
Give the single most important reason for your decision.

SECTION 2 — PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS
Analyze the candidate model's metrics in detail.
Are the numbers good for this type of model?
Is there anything concerning (e.g. high recall but low precision)?
Reference the actual metric values.

SECTION 3 — COMPARISON WITH PRODUCTION
How does the candidate compare to the current production model?
For each metric that changed, explain whether the change is meaningful
or within noise. Call out any regression (metric that got worse).
If this is the first deployment, state that clearly.

SECTION 4 — TREND ANALYSIS
Look at the recent run history.
Is the model improving over time, plateauing, or declining?
Any concerning patterns (e.g. validation loss increasing while
training loss decreases = overfitting)?

SECTION 5 — RISK FLAGS
List any specific risks with this model version.
Examples: data drift suspected, overfitting signals, significant
variance increase, underperformance on a specific metric.
If there are no risk flags, state "No risk flags identified."

SECTION 6 — RECOMMENDATION
State one of: PROMOTE TO STAGING | PROMOTE TO PRODUCTION | HOLD | REJECT
Give a one-sentence justification.
If recommending staging, specify whether canary (5%) or shadow deployment.
